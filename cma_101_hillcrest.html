101 Hillcrest Road — Agent Commentary

Based on four closed sales of Cape Cod style single-family homes in Glastonbury between August and December 2025, the estimated current market value for 101 Hillcrest Road falls in the range of $537,000 to $560,000. All four comparables sold above their list prices, with a sale-to-list ratio ranging from 107% to 115%, indicating strong buyer demand and limited inventory for this property type in this market.

The subject property's most significant differentiating feature is its 1.40-acre lot — the largest in this comparable set by a wide margin, where other lots ranged from 0.37 to 0.89 acres. 73 Water Street, which shares the same 1952 construction year, achieved a sold price of $550,000 despite having no garage. The subject's 2-car attached garage and public sewer connection place it favorably above that benchmark.

The highest-value comparable, 130 Overlook Road ($560,000), benefited from an extensively renovated interior and exterior, which is reflected in its higher $/sqft. The subject property's prior sale occurred in 2018 at $321,000, representing appreciation of approximately 67–74% to the current estimated range — consistent with broader Glastonbury market trends over that period.

Based on this analysis, 101 Hillcrest Road would most likely sell for approximately $549,000–$555,000.
